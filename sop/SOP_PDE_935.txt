SOP – PDE Error Code 935
-----------------------
 
Error Description:
Claim has already been adjudicated and submitted previously to CMS.
 
Objective:
Determine whether claim needs reprocessing or reporting only.
 
Resolution Steps:
 
1. Identify claims flagged with error code 935.
2. Compare Claim Received Date with Adjudication Timestamp.
3. If adjudication date is newer than received date:
   - Mark claim as already processed.
4. Do NOT reprocess the claim.
5. Include claim only in reporting output.
6. Exclude claim from PDE resubmission file.
7. Document decision for audit compliance.
 
Output:
- Claim excluded from reprocessing
- Report generated for already processed claims
- Audit record updated
